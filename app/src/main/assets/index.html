Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 | Effective for Edappadi Kadai App

🏢 About Us

Edappadi Kadai is operated by Coscoom Creative Tech Solutions, based in Idappadi, Salem, Tamil Nadu, India. We provide a hyperlocal fresh meat and vegetable delivery service through our mobile application.

📋 What Data We Collect

  • Name — To identify your account
  • Phone Number — For order confirmation and delivery coordination
  • Delivery Address / Location — To deliver your order to the correct place
  • Order History — To show your past orders and improve service
⚠️ We do NOT collect payment card details, Aadhaar, or any sensitive financial information.

🎯 Why We Collect It

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To contact you regarding your order status
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To resolve any delivery issues

🤝 Who We Share It With

Your data is shared only with:

  • Our delivery staff — for completing your order
  • Firebase (Google) — our secure cloud backend for data storage
✅ We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party advertisers or marketing companies.

🗄️ Data Storage & Retention

  • Data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days

⚖️ Legal Compliance

  • We comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • For users outside India: we follow applicable data protection regulations including GDPR principles

🔒 Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be notified through the app. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
